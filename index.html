Future Forms Masterclass

Mastering "Will", "Be Going To", and "Present Continuous"

1. Contextual Role-Playing 🎭

Rule Check: Use be going to for existing plans. Use will for spontaneous decisions. Use present continuous for fixed arrangements (like booked flights).

Exercise 1: The Vacation ✈️

You are planning a vacation. Write 3 sentences: one fixed arrangement, one plan, one spontaneous decision.

Exercise 2: The Restaurant 🍔

You are at a restaurant. You planned to eat a burger, but the waiter says they have fresh cake. Write a sentence for your original plan, and a new sudden decision.

Exercise 3: Weekend Changes ☔

You arranged to play tennis at 3 PM, but it suddenly starts raining. Cancel the arrangement and make a quick decision about what to do instead.

2. Information Gap 🗣️

Find a partner. Student A knows part of the story, Student B knows the rest. Write down the conversation you would have!

Exercise 1: Weekend Weather

Student A: You are visiting your grandparents this weekend.
Student B: You checked the news. It is going to rain all weekend.

Exercise 2: The Cinema Ticket

Student A: You are seeing the new action movie at 7 PM tonight.
Student B: You know the 7 PM movie is sold out.

Exercise 3: The Sick Friend

Student A: You are organizing a party. You plan to invite Sarah.
Student B: You just found out Sarah is very sick.

4. Debates & Discussions 💬

Practice speaking! Answer these open-ended questions out loud or write them down.

Discussion 1: Education (Predictions - Will)

Do you think AI and robots will replace teachers in 10 years? Why or why not?

Discussion 2: Personal Growth (Plans - Be going to)

What are you going to do to improve your English this year?

Discussion 3: Daily Life (Present Continuous)

What are you currently doing to prepare for your next holiday or weekend?

Discussion 4: The Environment (Predictions - Will / Be going to)

Look at the pollution in big cities. What is going to happen to the environment if we don't change? What will you do to help?

5. Sentence Transformation Drills 🔄

Rewrite the original sentence using the new forms. (Use full words or contractions like I'm)

1. She is traveling to London tomorrow.

2. I am playing tennis later.

3. They are meeting the boss at 6 PM.

4. He is starting a new job on Monday.

5. We are having pizza for dinner.

7. Interview Tasks 🎤

Interview a friend (or answer for yourself). Write complete sentences.

  1. What are you doing this evening? (Fixed plan)
  2. What are you going to do after this class? (Intention)
  3. Do you think it will rain later? (Prediction)
  4. Where are you traveling for your next holiday? (Arrangement)
  5. What will life be like in 2050? (Prediction)

8. Error Correction Game ❌✅

Find the mistakes in these 5 sentences and type the correct version.

1. Incorrect: I will going to the party tonight.

2. Incorrect: Look at those dark clouds! It will rain.

3. Incorrect: We fly to Madrid next Monday. We have tickets.

4. Incorrect: I am go to see a movie tomorrow.

5. Incorrect: She thinks he is going to wins the race.

9. Chain Stories 🔗

Let's build a story! For each starter, write two more sentences using different future forms.

Story 1: The Vacation

Teacher: Next summer, I’m going to visit Italy. (Plan)

Story 2: The Lottery

Teacher: I just won the lottery! I will buy a huge house. (Spontaneous decision)

Story 3: The Strange Noise

Teacher: Listen! Someone is knocking at the door. I will open it. (Spontaneous decision)
